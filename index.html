Built for Your Property. Designed for Your Lifestyle.

Chimas Construction provides professional concrete and hardscape services for residential properties throughout North Carolina. From durable driveways and walkways to beautiful patios, poolside concrete and paver installations, and retaining walls, we focus on quality workmanship and long-lasting results.

Our Services

Professional concrete and hardscape solutions designed to improve the beauty, function, and value of your property.

Concrete Driveways

Durable concrete driveways designed for everyday use. Choose from traditional non-stamped concrete or decorative stamped concrete finishes.

Concrete Patios & Outdoor Living

Create a comfortable outdoor space with a custom concrete patio. We offer both stamped and non-stamped concrete options.

Concrete Walkways

Professional concrete walkways that provide a clean, safe, and attractive path around your home. Available in stamped and non-stamped finishes.

Pool Surrounds & Poolside Hardscaping

Create a finished look around your in-ground pool with professionally installed stamped concrete, non-stamped concrete, or pavers. Poolside areas can be designed as a standalone project or connected to an existing patio or concrete area.

Paver Installation

Professional paver installation for patios, walkways, outdoor spaces, and other hardscape applications.

Retaining Walls

Strong and functional retaining wall solutions designed to improve the structure, appearance, and usability of your property.
